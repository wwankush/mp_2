Chowmein Recipe

Recipe Steps:

  1. Cook the Noodles: Boil water in a pot and cook the chowmein noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar. Set aside.

  3. Stir-Fry Vegetables: Heat cooking oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced onion and minced garlic. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

  4. Add Vegetables: Add the sliced bell pepper, julienned carrot, and thinly sliced cabbage to the pan. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

  5. Add Noodles and Sauce: Add the cooked noodles to the pan with the stir-fried vegetables. Pour the prepared sauce over the noodles and vegetables. Toss everything together until well combined. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

  6. Season and Garnish: Season the chowmein with salt and pepper to taste. If desired, garnish with sliced green onions for added freshness.

  7. Serve: Transfer the chowmein to serving plates or bowls. Serve hot and enjoy!

Extra Tips:

  • Prep Ingredients in Advance: Chop all your vegetables and prepare the sauce before you start cooking.

  • Use High Heat: Stir-frying works best with high heat, so make sure your pan or wok is hot before adding the ingredients.

  • Don't Overcook the Vegetables: Aim to stir-fry the vegetables until they are tender-crisp.

  • Cook Noodles Al Dente: Be careful not to overcook the noodles when boiling them.

  • Toss Frequently: Keep the ingredients moving in the pan or wok by tossing them frequently with a spatula or pair of tongs.

  • Adjust Seasonings to Taste: Taste the chowmein before serving and adjust the seasonings as needed.

  • Experiment with Add-Ins: Feel free to add your favorite proteins and additional vegetables to customize the dish.

  • Serve Immediately: Chowmein is best served hot and fresh from the pan.
