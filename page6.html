Chicken Wings Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon ginger powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions;

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Rinse the chicken wings under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together the soy sauce, honey, hoisin sauce, sesame oil, garlic powder, ginger powder, black pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  4. Add the chicken wings to the bowl and toss them until they are well coated with the marinade.
  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the wings marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, preferably overnight.
  6. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and place a wire rack on top. Arrange the marinated wings on the wire rack.
  7. Bake the wings in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, flipping them halfway through, until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  8. Remove the wings from the oven and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  9. Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions. Serve hot and enjoy!

Extra Tips:

  • For extra crispy wings, broil them for the last 2-3 minutes of cooking.
  • If you prefer spicier wings, increase the amount of red pepper flakes or add a dash of hot sauce to the marinade.
  • To ensure even cooking, make sure the wings are spread out in a single layer on the wire rack.
  • Serve with a side of ranch or blue cheese dressing for dipping.
  • You can also grill the wings instead of baking them for a smoky flavor.
