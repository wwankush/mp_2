Chicken Popcorn Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornmeal
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Place the chicken pieces in a large bowl and pour the buttermilk over them. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a separate large bowl, mix together the flour, cornmeal, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, black pepper, and salt.
  3. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk, allowing the excess to drip off. Dredge each piece in the seasoned flour mixture, ensuring an even coat. Shake off any excess flour.
  4. Heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil in a large, deep skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat until it reaches 350°F (175°C).
  5. Carefully add the chicken pieces to the hot oil in batches to avoid overcrowding the pan.
  6. Fry the chicken for 3-5 minutes, turning occasionally, until it is golden brown and crispy. The internal temperature should reach 165°F (74°C).
  7. Remove the chicken from the oil and place it on a wire rack set over a baking sheet to drain excess oil. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce and enjoy your delicious chicken popcorn!

Extra Tips:

  • For extra flavor, add a teaspoon of your favorite seasoning blend to the flour mixture.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, increase the amount of cayenne pepper or add a dash of hot sauce to the buttermilk marinade.
  • Make sure the oil temperature stays consistent. Too low, and the chicken will be greasy; too high, and it will burn before cooking through.
  • Use a thermometer to check the oil temperature and the internal temperature of the chicken for the best results.
  • Leftover chicken popcorn can be reheated in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for about 10-15 minutes to maintain its crispiness.
