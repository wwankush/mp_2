Fried Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 lbs chicken pieces (legs, thighs, wings, and breasts)
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the chicken pieces under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels.
  2. Place the chicken in a large bowl and pour the buttermilk over it. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  3. In a separate large bowl, mix together the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, black pepper, and salt.
  4. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk, allowing the excess to drip off. Dredge each piece in the seasoned flour mixture, ensuring an even coat. Shake off any excess flour.
  5. Heat about 1 inch of vegetable oil in a large, deep skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat until it reaches 350°F (175°C).
  6. Carefully add the chicken pieces to the hot oil, skin-side down. Fry in batches to avoid overcrowding the pan.
  7. Fry the chicken for 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until it is golden brown and crispy. The internal temperature should reach 165°F (74°C).
  8. Remove the chicken from the oil and place it on a wire rack set over a baking sheet to drain excess oil. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious fried chicken!

Extra Tips:

  • For extra crispy chicken, double-dip the chicken pieces: after the first coating of flour, dip them back in the buttermilk and then coat them again with the flour mixture.
  • If you prefer a spicier kick, add more cayenne pepper or a dash of hot sauce to the buttermilk marinade.
  • Make sure the oil temperature stays consistent. Too low, and the chicken will be greasy; too high, and it will burn before cooking through.
  • Use a thermometer to check the oil temperature and the internal temperature of the chicken for the best results.
  • Leftover fried chicken can be reheated in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for about 15-20 minutes to maintain its crispiness.
