Burger Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickle slices
  • Onion slices
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Mix gently until just combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty about 1/2 inch thick.
  4. Place the patties on the preheated grill or skillet. Cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. If using cheese, add a slice to each patty during the last minute of cooking and cover to melt.
  5. While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the burger buns on the grill or skillet until golden brown.
  6. Assemble the burgers by spreading mayonnaise, ketchup, and mustard on the bottom half of each bun.
  7. Place the cooked patties on the buns, then top with lettuce, tomato, pickles, and onions.
  8. Cover with the top half of the buns and serve immediately. Enjoy your delicious homemade burgers!

Extra Tips:

  • For juicier burgers, avoid over-mixing the ground beef. Mix just until the seasonings are incorporated.
  • Let the patties rest for a few minutes after shaping them. This helps them hold their shape better during cooking.
  • If you prefer a smoky flavor, add a dash of liquid smoke to the ground beef mixture.
  • Experiment with different types of cheese, such as cheddar, Swiss, or blue cheese, to find your favorite combination.
  • Serve the burgers with a side of fries or a fresh salad for a complete meal.
