Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. If using store-bought dough, let it come to room temperature as per the package instructions. If using homemade dough, roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Brush the dough with olive oil and sprinkle with garlic powder.
  5. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add the grated Parmesan cheese.
  7. Arrange the pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, black olives, and red onions evenly over the cheese.
  8. Sprinkle the dried oregano and dried basil over the toppings.
  9. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  10. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  11. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!

Extra Tips:

  • For a crispier crust, pre-bake the dough for 5 minutes before adding the toppings.
  • If you prefer a thinner crust, roll out the dough more thinly and reduce the baking time slightly.
  • Experiment with different cheeses like provolone, gouda, or feta for unique flavor combinations.
  • Add a drizzle of olive oil or balsamic glaze over the finished pizza for an extra touch of flavor.
  • Use a pizza stone for a more evenly cooked and crispy crust.
  • Customize your pizza with your favorite toppings, such as sausage, ham, pineapple, or spinach.
